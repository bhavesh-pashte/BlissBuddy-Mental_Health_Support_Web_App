BlissBuddy - Your Emotional Wellness Companion

Welcome to BlissBuddy – Your companion on the path to emotional well-being.

BlissBuddy is an innovative web application designed to empower you with insights into your emotional state. Using advanced facial emotion detection technology, our application analyzes your expressions in real-time. If a neutral or sad emotion is detected, BlissBuddy seamlessly guides you through a comprehensive PHQ9 survey.

Take a journey towards self-discovery as BlissBuddy assesses your mental well-being and generates a personalized PHQ-9 score. This score reflects your stress level and provides valuable insights into your emotional state. Our user-friendly interface ensures a seamless experience, offering guidance and support every step of the way.
